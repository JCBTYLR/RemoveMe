How BlockMe Works

  1. Real-time protection from over 800,000 annoying robocallers, telemarketers, and phone scammers
  2. Over 1,200+ new robocallers identified every day
  3. Important robocalls, like school closings and weather alerts, are able to get through
  4. Privacy friendly - Never accesses your call logs
  5. Identify or block robocalls (your choice)
  6. Protection from text spam and phishing attempts
  7. Listen to thousands of robocall messages in our database
  8. Block "neighbor spoofed" calls. These are numbers that are very similar to yours.

Volunteer and Donate

Earn BlockMe credits by volunteering your time. The BlockMe volunteer program allows you to earn in-app credits by only volunteering 15 minute blocks of time 8 times per week, two total hours. You will be rewarded in-app credits once you've completed the entire two hours. In-app credits are also available after donating to our page, please click the donate link for more information on donations.
